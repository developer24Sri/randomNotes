About us

Built for Learners.

Backed by Experts

Tricky & Solve is a student-first initiative dedicated to helping NEET-UG aspirants succeed with confidence. Since 2019, we've supported thousands of students through our physical mock test books, and now we're stepping into the digital space with affordable, high-quality e-books for Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.

Designed for self-paced learning and built with clarity in mind, our content is tailored to make complex concepts simple — so students can focus on what truly matters: understanding, revising, and excelling.

Empowering NEET Aspirants

Vision

To become the most trusted and affordable learning companion for NEET-UG aspirants within and across India, empowering every student—regardless of background or board—to dream big and achieve success through simplified, accessible, and high-quality study material.

Mission

"At Tricky & Solve, we bridge the gap between quality education and affordability. Since 2019, we’ve supported students with mock test books, and now with our first digital launch, we offer concise, exam-focused e-books in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry—making NEET-UG preparation smarter and more accessible."
